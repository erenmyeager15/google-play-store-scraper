# Google Play Store Scraper Promotion Notes

## Positioning

Promote this as an app metadata and optional review-row scraper for public Google Play listings.

Best-fit buyers:

- ASO teams.
- App-market researchers.
- Competitor intelligence teams.
- Product teams watching ratings, installs, versions, and pricing.

Avoid promising private user data or review-user identification. Reviews are public rows only when explicitly enabled.

## Short Video Ideas

1. "Export Google Play app metadata to CSV with Apify"
2. "Scrape Google Play ratings, installs, and prices by app ID"
3. "Compare Android app metadata across storefronts"

## Suggested Demo Flow

1. Open the Actor.
2. Use `com.openai.chatgpt`.
3. Keep reviews disabled and `maxResults: 1`.
4. Run the Actor.
5. Show rating, installs, price, version, and Play URL.
6. Mention review rows are optional and separately charged.

## Short Social Copy

Built a Google Play Store Scraper for Apify.

Input:
- package IDs
- keywords
- categories
- country/language

Output:
- app name and developer
- rating and review count
- installs and price
- version and Android requirement
- Play Store URL
- optional public review rows
